Prayers

Our heavenly Cage, Nick be thy name.

Your memedom come, your will be done, on Earth as it is on Reddit.

Give us today, our daily meme, and forgive us our reposting, as we forgive those who repost against us.

Lead us not to John Travolta, but deliver us from bees.

For thine is the faceswapping, forever and ever. Ramen.

Nicolas be thy name

And forgive him for Trespass,

For we forgave him for The Wicker Man,

And lead us not into Windtalkers

For thine is the National Treasure

For thee, my Cage, for thee

That our feet may swiftly carry out Thy command.
